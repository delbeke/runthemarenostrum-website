step 1: watch the explainer video

The Mare Nostrum has started 1th July 2019 in Knokke at the Dutch / Belgian border. During Leg One 20 teams of brave men and women have run via shores in Belgium, Picardy and Normandy to arrive in Portsall, Brittany at the very West of the Brittanic peninsula. Leg Two of the journey made us cross the most Western point of France moving more South landing us right after the peninsula of Quiberon.

We will continue our journey on the 1th May 2020 for Leg Three moving further South approaching Spain and we want you! To make sure we do not have gaps between the Stages and to form a continuous chain of runners we only open 10 Stages. Join the journey and co-create this unique challenge and book.

When you register for a Stage you will get a full day of support of our team along the way, a dedicated photo & video crew who will document your heroic efforts for eternity, a free Duvel at the end of the Stage and last but not least the printed book of the entire coast of Europe. Looking forward to have you on board.
