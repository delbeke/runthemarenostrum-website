During - Running
Goal: The ultimate goal of this journey is to create something magical together. Please be aware that the whole journey is a chain and that ... Self-Sufficient: As there will be no food or drink supply during the trail, nor guidelines or marks on the road it is critical to be fully prepared. We therefore advise to read the food guidelines and study the map and roadbook of your Stage. We expect participants to carry their own food and drinks but also the object and the photo as we want to keep quality and format of the pictures taken consistent. We estimate you to carry up to 8 kg (with 3kg water, 3kg food and 2kg electronics) Follow the Coast: As our goal is to follow and to map the outlines of the European coast we will follow certain rules of engagement. We will not take boats to cross waterways and we will always aim to run as close to the coast as possible.
